Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions answered about Stash.

What is Stash?
Stash is a study planner designed to help students organize, plan, and achieve their academic goals effectively.
Is Stash free to use?
Yes, Stash is completely free to use for all students.
Can I sync Stash with my calendar?
Yes, Stash allows you to sync your tasks and schedules with your Google Calendar for better organization.
Does Stash work offline?
While most features require an internet connection, basic functionalities like task viewing and editing are available offline.
How can I contact support?
You can contact support by visiting the Contact page and filling out the support form.
